Fisker

Two images made in photoshop and their potential usage


This first image was to highlight Fisker’s new EMotion car as an option that is both green without needing to sacrifice luxury. The background image is of a green mountain side, with fog rolling through. The car is double exposured to highlight its unique appearance, and to show its coexistence with green practices.


This second image was made to highlight the EMotion’s butterfly doors, a unique design aspect that makes it stands out from other competitors in the luxury electric car space.
